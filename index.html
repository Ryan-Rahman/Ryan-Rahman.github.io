About

RYAN RAHMAN is an author, who has written books such as The Boy, The Apple, And The Mom. He stays hidden in the dark corners, never being seen.

ILLUSTRATOR

ARVIND RAMESH is a new illustrator, not knowing that soon Night will soon destroy him with darkness. Of course, Night didn’t actually destroy him. That would be breaking rule #1,789,903 of The Rules Of Writing. He lives in Utah, with his family.
